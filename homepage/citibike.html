Citi Bike Website

Union Square is surrounded by Citi Bike stations, allowing for easy travel around it. Citi Bike is an effective way of travel as it provides access to thousands of bikes and stations around New York City and Jersey City. This service provide a convenient and eco-friendly mode of transportation to users as well as the community around them. This is a popular choice for tourists and locals as it's easily accesible. An example being accesibility to day passes over full memberships. The way the system works is that scanning a qr code on any bike from any station will unlock, able to be returned to any free station once the ride is finished. The use of bikes over other modes of transportation not only helps the environment, but the wellbeing of each rider as it promotes a healthier lifestyle full of physical activity.
